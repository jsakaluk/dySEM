# Ratings on items from the Positive-Negative Relationship Quality Scale (PNRQ; Rogge et al., 2017) from 219 (M)ixed-sex couples

A data set containing ratings on items (4 each) assessing romantic (1)
satisfaction and (2) dissatisfaction for each member of a mixed-sex dyad
. Positive-Negative Relationship Quality Scale (Rogge et al., 2017).
Data are from Prine et al. (Under Review). Variable names follow a
stem-item-partner ("sip") order, with a delimiter ("\_") between the
item number and distinguishing partner character.

## Usage

``` r
data(pnrqM)
```

## Format

A data frame with 219 rows and 16 variables:

- sat.pnrq1_w:

  Satisfaction item 1 for partner 1

- sat.pnrq2_w:

  Satisfaction item 2 for partner 1

- sat.pnrq3_w:

  Satisfaction item 3 for partner 1

- sat.pnrq4_w:

  Commitment item 1 for partner 1

- dsat.pnrq1_w:

  Commitment item 2 for partner 1

- dsat.pnrq2_w:

  Commitment item 3 for partner 1

- dsat.pnrq3_w:

  Intimacy item 1 for partner 1

- dsat.pnrq4_w:

  Intimacy item 2 for partner 1

- sat.pnrq1_m:

  Intimacy item 3 for partner 1

- sat.pnrq2_m:

  Trust item 1 for partner 1

- sat.pnrq3_m:

  Trust item 2 for partner 1

- sat.pnrq4_m:

  Trust item 3 for partner 1

- dsat.pnrq1_m:

  Passion item 1 for partner 1

- dsat.pnrq2_m:

  Passion item 2 for partner 1

- dsat.pnrq3_m:

  Passion item 3 for partner 1

- dsat.pnrq4_m:

  Love item 1 for partner 1

## References
